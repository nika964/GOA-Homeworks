halloween

Halloween is a fun and spooky celebration that takes place on October 31st.
People of all ages dress up in costumes, ranging from ghosts and goblins to superheroes and celebrities.
The tradition of trick-or-treating involves children going door-to-door to collect candy from neighbors.
Homes and streets are often decorated with pumpkins, cobwebs, and eerie lights to create a haunted atmosphere.
Many people enjoy carving pumpkins into jack-o-lanterns,
which are placed outside to greet visitors. Halloween also brings about themed parties, scary movies, and fun activities,
making it one of the most anticipated events of the year.
